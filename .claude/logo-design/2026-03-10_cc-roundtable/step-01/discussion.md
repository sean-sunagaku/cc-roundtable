# Step 01 - Team Discussion Summary

## Participants
- brand-strategist
- logo-designer
- color-type-expert
- trend-researcher
- competitive-analyst

## Key Consensus Points

### Visual Direction
- **Circular/radial arrangement** is the strongest differentiator — no competitor owns this space
- **"Roundtable" metaphor** maps perfectly to product concept and is universally understood (Western: King Arthur equality; Japanese: enso/wa)
- **Bold geometry** over thin lines — must work at 16px
- **Abstract over literal** — avoid medieval imagery, AI cliches, chat bubbles

### Top 5 Concepts (from brand-strategist + logo-designer synthesis)

1. **"The Gathering Ring"** — Bold ring with 5 agent dots on perimeter
2. **"Code Brackets Circle"** — Curly brackets `{ }` curved into a circular form with discussion dot
3. **"Linked CC"** — Two interlocked "c" letterforms as chain links
4. **"Discussion Orbit"** — Central dot + 3 orbiting arc segments suggesting rotation
5. **"Table View"** — Top-down solid table with 4 seats and `< >` center mark

### Color Direction
- **Recommended: Slate Teal palette** — Primary `#0D9488` (teal) + Accent `#F59E0B` (amber)
- Teal is genuinely unclaimed in dev tool space
- Balances technical trust (blue-adjacent) with collaborative warmth (green-adjacent)
- Alternatives: Deep Indigo (`#4F46E5` + `#10B981`) or Warm Graphite (`#374151` + `#DC2626`)

### Typography
- **Recommended: JetBrains Mono + Inter** — mono for "cc", sans-serif for "roundtable"
- Developer-native recognition
- Excellent small-size readability

### Competitive Differentiation
- No competitor uses circular/radial multi-node arrangement
- Avoid: orange/amber (Claude), blurple (Discord), teal gradient (Windsurf), purple (Linear/Devin)
- Unclaimed: deep green, warm gold + cool accent, earth tones
- The "multiple agents gathered in a circle" concept is unique in the landscape

### Trend Alignment
- High-impact minimalism (must-have)
- Bold geometry with circle/ring as core (must-have)
- Warm non-blue palette (must-have for differentiation)
- Motion-ready design (nice-to-have)
- Console/mono aesthetic for wordmark (medium priority)

### Risks Identified
- "Gathering Ring" could read as generic group/network icon if dots too small
- "Code Brackets" could be misread as loading spinner
- "Linked CC" might not immediately convey "meeting/collaboration"
- "Discussion Orbit" could look like atom/science imagery
- "Table View" has too many elements for 16px
