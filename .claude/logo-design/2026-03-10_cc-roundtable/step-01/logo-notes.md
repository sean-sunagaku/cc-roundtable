# Step 1 - Logo Notes

## V1: The Gathering Ring
- **File**: logos/v1-gathering-ring.svg
- **Concept**: Ring with 5 dots = agents at roundtable
- **Good**: Clear roundtable metaphor, scalable, simple
- **Bad**: No "room" feeling. Feels like a network diagram, not a space
- **Improve**: Add enclosure/boundary to create room feeling
- **Keep?**: △ Element (dots around circle) is useful but needs room context

## V2: Code Brackets Circle
- **File**: logos/v2-code-brackets-circle.svg
- **Concept**: Curly brackets curved into circle with spark
- **Good**: Code-native, unique shape
- **Bad**: No room/space feeling at all. Reads as pure code symbol
- **Improve**: Could brackets form walls of a room?
- **Keep?**: △ Bracket idea interesting but needs spatial grounding

## V3: Linked CC
- **File**: logos/v3-linked-cc.svg
- **Concept**: Two interlocked "c" letterforms
- **Good**: Brand name integration, minimal
- **Bad**: No room feeling. No meeting/gathering feeling. Just letters
- **Improve**: Could "cc" be inside or form a room?
- **Keep?**: × Direction doesn't support "room" concept

## V4: Discussion Orbit
- **File**: logos/v4-discussion-orbit.svg

- **Keep?**: △ Rotation concept interesting but needs room reframe

## V5: Table View
- **File**: logos/v5-table-view.svg

- **Improve**: Add room boundary/enclosure around the table
- **Keep?**: ○ Best foundation for "room" direction — develop further
